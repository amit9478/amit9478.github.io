We have defined a process which works best for delivering useful products in reasonable time. Inspired from Agile, Design Thinking and GV, our 5 days iterative design sprint is what makes us special.

Learn

We learn everything about your business, industry and user by conducting various design activities. Designing user-friendly interfaces need a clear understanding of peopleâ€™s behavior and context of use. We posses expertise in various user driven lean research methodologies.

Ideate

After the initial learning phase, our team conducts various brainstorming & brain writing sessions. These sessions helps us in forming meaningful patterns. These patterns help us to come up with various concepts for the target user. We do storyboarding and note down every scenario which user will encounter while using designed product or service.

Prototyping

We believe that prototyping is the only method to arrive at concrete and valuable user experience. We use lo to hi fidelity iterative process of prototyping for designing interfaces. At this stage we define navigation, put layers of content including its presentation and interaction for users.

Evaluate

Once we are through with prototyping stage, we go to the users again to validate designed solution. At this stage we look for user acceptance, usage benefits and drawbacks in various contexts. A simple user test uncovers valuable insights very quickly.
