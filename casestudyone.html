designed a native mobile app for live broadcasting

The Challenge

Engaging users

2016 was the year when video consumption on mobile devices was increasing rapidly. This new trend in video consumption created enough space for new video based digital products.


On the similar lines this solution catered to Bollywood centric content creation & consumption. Various celebrities were going live on mobile app and users were interacting with them in the form of live chats.

Approach

Learn. Ideate. Prototype. Test

We started with stakeholder interview for understanding their product vision, & target users. Various rounds of initial concepts and lo fidelity prototyping were done.


We moved from lo fidelity wire-frames to High fidelity wire-frames with in 2 design sprints of one week each. Every scenario was laid out and various flows were developed for user's engagement.


Usability Testing

Refining Solution

We conducted usability testing in in iterative approach. Initial usability sessions were designed to capture user needs and their expectations from the mobile app. In further rounds of usability testing we evluated the designed interfaces by including task specific usability parameters.


Usability sessions gave us enough insights to improve the solution further and engage them more with the application.

Designs

Bringing all to life

Once we had all the insights from usability testing, we improved existing designs. More usability testing session were conducted with real users before delivering the final solution.
