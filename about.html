Our Team

Swapnil Arora

Founder & UX Designer

Swapnil has been creating outstanding user experiences for more than 8 years. She is a NID alumnus with experience across multiple domains including Banking & Finance, Healthcare, Telecom, Retail & Security and others. She has a zest to create an environment where she and team can innovate and achieve their best. She creates great experiences working collaboratively with fellow team members to ideate, design and prototype ideas to design user friendly products.
She is a lover of simple & effective designs which can make people life easier.

Amit Panwar

Co-Founder & UX Designer

Amit is an HFI certified usability analyst who has 8+ years of experience as a UX Designer. He has worked on small scale to large scale digital products spread across domains like e-Commerce, banking & finance, Security etc. For him designing is all about finding and solving user's problem. He is well versed designer and has hands on experience on delivering simple to use, useful and usable products. He has the knack of leveraging available insights like market analysis, customer feedback, site metrics, and usability findings and uses these in solving design problems.
After he has worked for many hours straight, he does not think of hitting the sack; - rather one will shot of ‘biking around the town’ is enough to get him plugged for another day’s work.
